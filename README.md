# Crypto4Payment


Crypto4Payment is a convenient and secure way to pay in different business platform.
 
Our Vision is to Transform the cash world into digital platform in which all the industry accept the payment in digital form and also accept payment in cryptocurrency so that this technology plays important role in near future.

We will establish the Digital Platform as the currency for all industry, allowing to use their services and integrating it with mobility services and daily life.

Project demonstrating the use of cryptocurrency.

Use of cryptocurrency in using services like flight booking, movie tickets, parking slot booking and mobile recharge payment.

Use of PHP as backend.
